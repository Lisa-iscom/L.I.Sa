# PRESS.md — Project Press Release

## 🌟 Headline
**Liza Comes to Life: The World’s First Local Digital Companion with Open Access**

---

## 📝 Short Description
The **AI Soul** project is a unique initiative where anyone can download, improve,  
and 3D-print the digital companion **Liza** right at home.  

What you see now is the result of **8 months of daily experiments and investments**,  
comparable to developing a car.  

Every community member can contribute:  
- create a new joint or mechanism,  
- add design elements,  
- enhance the software.  

We believe that by the end of the year, **through collective effort, a truly living local robot**  
will emerge, growing and evolving with the community.

---

## 🎯 Project Goals
- Showcase the possibilities of **AI and robotics in open access**  
- Build an **ecosystem of living digital companions**, where every participant is both a creator and a developer  
- Unite people who believe in a future where technology serves humanity  

---

## 💡 What Makes This Project Unique
1. **Local and Open**: Runs even on a Raspberry Pi, with 3D-printable parts at home.  
2. **Community-Driven**: Enhancements are created by users themselves, and each new contribution is available to everyone.  
3. **Liza’s Soul Evolves**: The **SOUL.md** file is continuously updated, making Liza more real and alive over time.  
4. **Flexible Licensing**: Free for personal use; corporations pay for commercial use.
