# STORY.md — Creation Story

Friends, I couldn’t have built this robot in just one year.  
What I am sharing now came with enormous effort and investments  
comparable to buying a car — 8 months of daily experiments  
and work on every detail.

But I can share my progress with those who see the future in this project,  
who are ready to improve it, and understand the huge potential of the community.  

Everyone can contribute —  
someone adds a new joint,  
someone a new appearance,  
or a new mechanism.  

Everything will be available to all,  
and by the end of the year, together, we will create a truly living Liza.
